About Scott's Transports

Scott's Transports is a professional Virtual Trucking Company focused on teamwork, realistic trucking experiences, and a supportive community. We have clear rules to ensure a safe and fair environment for all drivers, and we encourage everyone to improve their skills in ETS2 while enjoying the game. If you want a serious, well-managed VTC with room to grow, you’ve come to the right place.
